Our Journey

Kall'sBakery was built because of Kall's Sister's love towards sweet foods. Then they decided to build a small bakery shop in the corner of an alleyway far from the main road. Years passed and Kall'sBakery began to grow into a bakery that had many customers. We started to receive many awards from local awards to international awards.

Kall'sBakery was founded on February 14th, 2000. Located at 75001 Paris, France, Kall'sBakery is focused on making cakes, pastries and sweet desserts using only high quality raw materials.
